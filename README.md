## Founding principles

1. **No paid ads, ever.** Product quality and word-of-mouth are the only allowed growth channels. We don't believe paying for attention is a healthy long-term position.
2. **Sovereign data.** Quebec Loi 25 / GDPR / SOC 2 by design. Customer data lives in Canadian regions only.
3. **AI-augmented, not AI-imitating.** Every AI-generated piece of content is clearly attributed, citation-grounded, and the human owner is named.
4. **Open knowledge graph.** Internal Manera petals cross-cite each other. Customers see exactly which signal came from which source.
5. **Reverse the buyer-seller asymmetry.** In Bidit, vendors compete for customers, not the other way around. Same principle applies to Manera Intel: customers ask, the mesh proposes — they don't get pitched.
